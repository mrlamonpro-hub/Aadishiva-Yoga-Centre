Our Practice

One studio, two disciplines that were never really separate.

Named for Adiyogi — the first teacher of yoga — Aadishiva was built on a simple idea: strength and stillness train the same body. Our instructors hold certifications across hatha, vinyasa and pranayama, and our floor is equipped for real strength conditioning, not just stretching.

Every session at Aadishiva opens with breath. Whether you're here for a 6 AM mysore-style flow or an evening strength circuit, we start the same way — because how you breathe decides how you move.
